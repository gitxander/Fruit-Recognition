NUTRIENTS

Rambutan contains phosphorus, iron, protein, fat, vitamin C, carbohydrates, Calcium, Potassium, B12, Magnesium, Vitamin A, Naicin. Rambutan skins also contains tannins and saponins.



BENEFITS

-Dysentery treatment using sufficient rambutan skin
-Nourish Hair
-Healing fever
-Diabetes treatment



MAJOR PRODUCTION

Tarlac,Batangas and Laguna

TYPE OF FRUIT

Native and Tropical Fruit


KIND OF FRUITS

A broad-topped tree with dark-red fruits having uncrowded spines 3/5 in(1.5cm) long ,and grayish-white, tough, subacid flesh 1/5 in thick

It seperates readily from seed but with a greater part of the seed coat adhering to flesh. Seed is oval to oblong and is compressed laterlly.
