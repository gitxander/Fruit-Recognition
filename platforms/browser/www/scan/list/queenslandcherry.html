NUTRIENTS

The edible portion is 65-80 % of the fruit. Per 100g the edible portion contains; water 90-95 g, carbohydrates 6.3 g, fat 0.8 g, protein 0.7 g, calcium 37-120 mg, phosphorus 22-40 mg, iron 0.7 mg, vitamin C 8mg, and vitamin A 10 IU. The energy value is 134 KJ/ 100g. Citric acid is the predominant organic acid.
